What is Steganography?

Steganography is the practice of concealing information within other non-secret data or a physical object. Unlike encryption, which makes data unreadable, steganography hides the existence of the secret communication itself.

Our application employs modern steganographic techniques to provide secure communications while maintaining the appearance of normal, innocent files.
